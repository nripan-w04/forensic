# Forensic Management Platform: System Architecture & Role Features

## 1. Administrator
The Oversight Authority with Level-5 system clearance.

### Sidebar Features
*   **Dashboard (Dynamic Data)**: Displays real-time system statistics such as total cases, active/closed cases, personnel count, and recent system activities via a high-tech "Command Center" interface.
*   **Personnel (Manage)**: Allows Admin to view all registered personnel, approve or reject new registrations (Personnel Registry), and manage access levels across the department.
*   **Case Files (Summary)**: Provides a comprehensive workload audit and summarized view of all case records, including investigation status and overall progress.
*   **Evidence Vault (View)**: Enables the Admin to monitor all stored evidence records, tracking their status from collection to analysis and court presentation.

### Navbar Features
*   **Logout**: Securely terminates the oversight session and logs the administrator out.

---

## 2. Police Department
Investigation and Field Operations unit.

### Sidebar Features
*   **Dashboard (Dynamic Values)**: Shows assigned cases, investigation updates, pending tasks, and recent dispatch activity in the "Investigation Terminal".
*   **Active Cases (Investigation Review)**: Allows officers to add investigation notes, update suspect lists, and manage ongoing case details (edit/delete functionality included).
*   **Register Case**: Comprehensive form to initiate new case files with FIR numbers, incident details, and primary suspects.
*   **Evidence Registry**: Displays all evidence linked to investigations, showing real-time status updates from the forensic lab.
*   **Profile**: Dedicated portal for officers to update their professional credentials and personal details.

### Navbar Features
*   **Logout**: Securely ends the investigation session.

---

## 3. Lab Analyst
Forensic Science and Evidence Analysis unit.

### Sidebar Features
*   **Dashboard (Dynamic Values)**: Displays high-level metrics for pending evidence items, completed analyses, and AI-assisted diagnostic results.
*   **Queue (Analysis & Reporting)**: The core workflow area where analysts access evidence records, perform detailed forensic tests, and upload digital reports.
*   **Profile**: Portal to manage analyst credentials and personal information.

### Navbar Features
*   **Logout**: Terminates the laboratory access session.

---

## 4. Evidence Collector
Chain of Custody and Field Evidence unit.

### Sidebar Features
*   **Dashboard (Dynamic Values)**: Tracks assigned collection tasks, recently processed items, and pending actions in the field.
*   **Link to Case**: Specialized view to associate newly collected evidence with existing FIR/Case records.
*   **Manage Evidence**: Central registry to update collection details, track physical locations, and manage barcode/QR associations.
*   **Profile**: Update personal and contact details for field coordination.

### Navbar Features
*   **Logout**: Securely logs out of the evidence management system.

---

## 5. Court Official
Judicial Oversight and Verdict Recording unit.

### Sidebar Features
*   **Dashboard (Dynamic Values)**: Provides summaries of cases filed in court, upcoming hearing schedules, and recent judicial activities.
*   **Hearings (Schedule & Records)**: Interface to set hearing dates, view forensic evidence reports, and record preliminary court findings.
*   **Judgments (Verdict Recording)**: Secure portal to record final verdicts, legal notes, and officially close case records.
*   **Profile**: Manage official judicial credentials and contact information.

### Navbar Features
*   **Logout**: Securely terminates the judicial oversight session.
